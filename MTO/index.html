Causal Inference

1

Treatment Effect

We will compare the earnings of 500 individuals, 250 growing up in Van Dyke Public Housing and 250 growing up in Nehemiah Houses.

In this simulation, the only reason that different people earn different amounts is either because of their neighborhood or their race.

The causal effect of growing up in the Nehemiah Houses compared with growing up in the Van Dyke Public Housing is $2,000.

That is, if you grow up in the Nehemiah Houses, you’d make $2000 more than you’d make if you grew up in the Van Dyke Public Housing.
