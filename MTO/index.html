Treatment Effect

In this simulation, In this simulation, we will compare the earnings of 500 individuals, 250 growing up in the MLK Towers and 250 growing up in Wakefield in the Bronx.

In this simulation, the only reason that different people earn different amounts is either because of their neighborhood or their race.

The causal effect of growing up in the Wakefield in the Bronx compared with growing up in Martin Luther King Jr. Towers in Harlem is $2000.

That is, if you grow up in Wakefield, you’d make $2000 more than you’d make if you grew up in the Martin Luther King Jr. Towers.
